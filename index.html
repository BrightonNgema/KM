About Us

Keeya Marketing Group (KMG)Trading As Key Marketing is a Johannesburg based company. We intend to provide innovative services whereby we represent the interest of our clients to our maximum capacity for assurance and retaining them.
As a Dynamic Group we administer incoming product support or information inquiries from clientele consumers including selling and marketing products and services, to achieve this we provide design, marketing, promotion and advertising support to the consortium, and potentially individual members, as required. All aspects of the consortium’s marketing and communications, from the brand/corporate identity to advertising campaigns .

KEY Marketing applies one element of marketing: the 4Ps of price, product, promotion and place, Promotion communicates the benefits of products or actions to potential and current customers. It can be very general, as an advertising campaign however, it can also be very specific, such as a “buy two, get one free” sales promotion.

Services

Self Promotions
